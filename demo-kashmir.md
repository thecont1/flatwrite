### DAY 1: July 17 (Wednesday)

Arrive in **Srinagar** early in the morning and finish airport formalities (for foreign nationals). Check-in to **Blooming Dale Hotel** with a refreshing cup of Kashmiri *kahwah*. A great way to start your impression of Kashmir. After rest and lunch, we will head to one of the exquisite Mughal gardens, **Chashme Shahi**. Maybe watch the sunset from **Pari Mahal**, a seven-terraced garden located at the top of the Zabarwan mountain range overlooking the city of Srinagar. Overnight at Blooming Dale Hotel.

### DAY 2: July 18 (Thursday)

Excursion to Sonamarg ('the meadow of gold'), a hill station in Ganderbal district. This golden valley is carved on the edges of the mighty River Sindh. Take a pony ride (Optional) or a half-hour walk to reach the Thajiwas Glacier, a major attraction in the summer months. Return to Srinagar and overnight at Blooming Dale Hotel.

### DAY 3: July 19 (Friday)

Day trip to Pahalgam, 'the village of shepherds' situated around the superb Lidder river in the Anantnag district. On the way, stop to visit the historic ruins of Awantipora temple and the Martand temple (dedicated to Surya the Sun God). Along the way, see fields of saffron and the cricket bat makers. In Pahalgam, visit Chandanwari and Betab Valley and/or 'mini Switzerland' Baisaran Valley. Overnight at Hotel Alpine K2.

### DAY 4: July 20 (Saturday)

Drive back to Srinagar for a day of sightseeing. Mr. Firdous will take us on a personal tour of his Srinagar. Visit the great Mughal gardens - Nishat Bagh and/or Shalimar Bagh. Drive through downtown Srinagar (Shehr-e-Khaas), enjoy the views of the dense city from one of the kadals (bridges) on the Jhelum river, and stop at historic monuments such as Jamia Masjid and Khanqah-e- Moula. Evening shopping of Kashmiri handicrafts/handlooms on the boulevard. Overnight at Hotel Blooming Dale.

### DAY 5: July 21 (Sunday)

In the morning, we will visit Shankaracharya Hill for divine views of Srinagar and (Optional) seek His divine blessings at the temple. A laid-back wazwan (feast) at the famous Ahdoos Hotel (Optional). Perfect time to spend an afternoon exploring the Dal Lake on a cosy shikara (hand-paddled boats with cushioned seating). Visit the peaceful Char Chinari island, Nehru Park, Nigeen Lake, and row through the floating gardens known as "Rad" in Kashmiri which blossom with lotus flowers in July and August. Evening shopping of Kashmiri natural products. Overnight at Hotel Blooming Dale.

**Optional:** Spend the night on a shikara. Tea and freshly cooked dinner will be served. Wake up to a bustling vegetable market on the lake.

Check out of Hotel Blooming Dale after breakfast and head to the hill station of Gulmarg ('the meadow of flowers') in Baramulla district. Check into the Royal Park Hotel and freshen up for mesmerizing visuals of Gulmarg. Visit to Golf Club, Strawberry Valley, Nageen Valley and Maharaja Palace. Take the highest cable rope car in the world, famously known as Gondola. Proceed to Phase 2 of the gondola (Optional - sometimes closed due to low oxygen levels!) at a height of 12,293 feet asl for mesmerising views of the Khilanmerg and Afarwat glaciers. Overnight at the Royal Park Hotel.
